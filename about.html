Hi, I'm Nick

I'm a copywriter and marketing consultant living in the beautiful Pacific Northwest. I've been writing for the web in some form ever since I had to disconnect my modem to use the phone.

Over the course of my career I've worked in sales, marketing, technology and everything in between. One thing I learned is that there's poor, ineffective writing all around.

So what is good copy?

Good copy moves a person to action.

Or as John McIntyre says:

“Copywriting is using words to persuade people to take some kind of action, such as clicking on an ad or purchasing a product. For this reason, it’s one of the most important aspects of marketing..."
